Comparative Analysis of Sandboxing and Mitigation Philosophies in Mobile User-Agent Architectures

July 2026 · PDF · Independent Security Research

A multi-layered threat-modeling analysis of GeckoView and Chromium security architectures on Android, examining process isolation, memory safety, extension infrastructure, privacy convergence, and monoculture risk. Includes a claim-by-claim critical examination of GrapheneOS's published advisories against Gecko-based browsers.

About

This site publishes research on mobile browser security. The current paper evaluates security claims made by hardened Android platforms against Gecko-based browsers, grounded in publicly available primary sources and current as of July 2026.
