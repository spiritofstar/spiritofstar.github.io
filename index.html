Paper

Comparative Analysis of Sandboxing and Mitigation Philosophies in Mobile User-Agent Architectures

July 2026 · PDF · 18 pages · CC BY 4.0

A multi-layered threat-modeling analysis comparing GeckoView and Chromium security architectures on Android. Examines process isolation, Rust memory safety, extension infrastructure, privacy convergence with security, and monoculture risk. Includes a claim-by-claim critical examination of GrapheneOS's published advisories against Gecko-based browsers.

About

Independent security research focused on mobile browser architectures. This paper evaluates claims made by hardened Android platforms against Gecko-based browsers, grounded in publicly available primary sources and current as of July 2026.
